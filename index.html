Quiz

Quiz is a form of assessment, often a game or competition, where participants answer questions to test their knowledge or skills. Quizzes can cover a wide range of topics and be used for various purposes, from educational assessments to entertainment. They can be simple or complex, short or long, and utilize different question formats like multiple-choice, true/false, or short answer. .


Doodle

"doodle" can refer to two main things: a simple drawing, often done absentmindedly, or an online scheduling tool. Doodles as drawings can range from abstract patterns to recognizable figures and are typically created while the mind is occupied with something else. Doodle as a website is a tool used for scheduling meetings and coordinating schedules.

Treasure Hunt

A treasure hunt is a game where participants follow a series of clues to find a hidden prize. These clues can be riddles, maps, or other forms of guidance that lead to the final treasure. Treasure hunts are a fun activity that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and skill levels, and they can be tailored to different environments and themes.
